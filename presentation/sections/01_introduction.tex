\begin{frame}{Introduction}{What will be presented today?}

The presentation time for a bachelor thesis, master thesis or research project is normally \qty{30}{\minute}.

Since it is a good guidline to have \textbf{MAXIMUM 1 slide per Minute} this should also limit the number of slides you present to 30.

Obviously you can animate your slides within \LaTeX.

See \texttt{example-main.tex} for formating hints. It is a copy of the official template of the FAU.


The sections within this set of slides are just a hint on how a talk should be structured.

The introduction should provide the context of this talk: Is it a master thesis? Who initialized it? Etc.
\end{frame}
